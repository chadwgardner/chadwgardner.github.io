Religion & Atheism Posts

Reverse Chronological Order

Thinking about religion has been a central theme of both my private life and my graduate school studies. A central arc of my life was transitioning from a Catholic who thought within the confines of a scientifically oriented theology to an atheist scholar who thinks about the historical contexts of the very categories of "science" and "religion" themselves. I started my first real blog toward the end of my stint in graduate school, and most of the posts here come from that time period. My thinking has evolved in many ways since then, but I've preserved these posts in mostly their original forms (with a few parenthetical asides throughout).
